ü§ù Community Guidelines

  • Respect privacy - What's shared in the group stays in the group
  • Be supportive and non-judgmental - Everyone's journey is unique
  • Listen actively and share thoughtfully - Give others space to be heard
  • Respect different perspectives and experiences
  • No hate speech or discriminatory behavior - Zero tolerance for harmful language
  • Seek professional help when needed - Peer support complements but doesn't replace professional help
  • Be kind and compassionate - We're all here to support each other
  • Report concerns - If you see something, say something
